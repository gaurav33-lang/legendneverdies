ABOUT US

This digital museum was created by me, Gaurav Kunwar — a young student from Butwal, Nepal learning about cyber security and deeply interested in farming. I wanted to honor our great heroes and martyrs who sacrificed everything for freedom, unity, and justice. Their stories deserve to be remembered forever.

OUR VISION

To keep the eternal flame of sacrifice burning in the hearts of every generation.
To ensure no hero is forgotten, no martyr silenced.
To remind the world that freedom is written in blood and preserved in memory.
